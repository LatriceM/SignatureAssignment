Available Services

Below is a comprehensive list of support services available in Boston to make your stay more convenient and comfortable.

  1. Transportation

    Getting around Boston is easy with these transportation options:

    • Subway: 6:00 AM - 11:30 PM
    • Buses: 5:30 AM - 12:00 AM

    The MBTA subway system, known locally as the "T," is the most efficient way to navigate Boston. Buses cover additional areas not serviced by the subway.

  2. Maintenance

    If you need maintenance services during your stay:

    • Plumbing: 24/7 Emergency Services
    • Electrical: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM

    For emergency maintenance in hotels or rental properties, contact your front desk or property manager first. They can connect you with the appropriate services.

  3. Cleaning Services

    Keep your accommodations clean and tidy:

    • Hotel Housekeeping: Daily 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
    • Office Cleaning: Weekdays 7:00 PM - 12:00 AM

    Most hotels offer daily housekeeping services. For extended stays or rental properties, ask about weekly cleaning services that may be available.
